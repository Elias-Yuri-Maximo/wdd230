Target Audience

Description

The target audience of the website are people that are interested in knowing the weather in general. Maybe for traveling, going on a hike or camp, or just general curiosity. It will probably be more often frequented by people who like to go outdoors. Including mainly people from the of 18 to 50, from all sorts of social classes. It will be also visited by people who want to travel, which opens a wider age demographic. And people who are simply curious about it will eventually visit the website to receive information.

Five Scenarios

  1. What will be the temperature in my city today? Should I buy an air conditioning?
  2. I want to go on a hike on the weekend, will the weather be good for it?
  3. I have heard that the storm was pretty bad in preston, where can I have some news about it?
  4. I want to walk my dog, is it a good day for it?
  5. I have very sensitive skin, is today a good day for me to go out? or is too sunny?
