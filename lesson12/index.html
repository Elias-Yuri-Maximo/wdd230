Our carnival

 The City Hall of Campinas (SP) released the schedule of the street carnival blocks. In all, there will be 30 blocks distributed throughout the municipality, including the districts of Barão Geraldo, Sousas and Joaquim Egídio. For the third year in a row, the municipality will not have samba school parades. See the full schedule below.
 The celebrations begin on February 2nd with the "Cordão do Félix" block, in Praça Carlos Gomes. A novelty this year is the merger of the "Nem Sangue Nem Areia" block with the Short Fair, in Vila Industrial.
 To monitor traffic and block lanes, agents from the Campinas Municipal Development Company (Emdec) will accompany the participants through the city's streets, as well as ambulances from the Mobile Emergency Service (Samu) will be on standby in case of accidents.

Local Fairs

 With the pandemic, changes in the Food Trucks market and in all sectors were inevitable, for the sector of food and beverages in general, it was also no different.
 Many companies traditionally opt for the well-known buffet to serve food at events: Who has never been to a corporate event, especially at the end of the year with the following configuration: barbecue, a large table with side dishes, drinks in a large ice box?
 However, with the arrival of the pandemic and now with the resumption of events little by little, the self-service format is no longer viable, as the risk of contamination is greater, even following all safety protocols.
